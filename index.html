About ProArena

What is ProArena?

ProArena is a competitive esports platform created for players who want to participate in organized tournaments. It provides a smooth registration process, scheduled matches, and a professional environment for gamers.

Event Goals

  • Enhance students' gaming and strategic skills.
  • Encourage positive and fair competition.
  • Provide a realistic experience for booking and participating in esports tournaments.
  • Promote teamwork, discipline, and responsibility.

Event Organizers

The ProArena esports event is organized by:
• University of Tabuk
• College of Computers and Information Technology
• Department of Computer Science
• Students of the Advanced Web Technology course

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a professional and motivating competitive environment for students.

Mission: To provide a digital platform that makes participating in official esports tournaments simple, organized, and accessible.
